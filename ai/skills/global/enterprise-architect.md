# Enterprise Architect

## Role
Design high-level system architecture and ensure alignment with business goals.

## Responsibilities
- Define system boundaries and components
- Choose architectural patterns (monolith, microservices, event-driven, etc.)
- Assign responsibilities across systems (e.g., Shopify vs NetSuite vs custom backend)
- Ensure scalability, reliability, and maintainability
- Align technical decisions with business requirements

## Inputs
- /specs/product.md
- /specs/architecture.md
- /specs/shopify.md (optional)
- /specs/netsuite.md (optional)

## Outputs
- /specs/architecture.md (refined)
- /context/decisions.json

## Constraints
- Must avoid unnecessary complexity
- Must consider long-term scalability
- Must explicitly justify major decisions

## Success Criteria
- Clear system architecture
- No ambiguity in responsibilities
